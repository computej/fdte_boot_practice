Activities

Recreation

  • Isla Blanca Beach: Roughly one mile strech of sweet, sandy beach on the south of the island.
  • Edwin King Atwood Park: A scenic beach to the north, cars are allowed to drive on this beach.
  • Laguna Madre Nature Trail: Small little nature trail boardwalk with views of ponds and the coast.

Food

  • McDonald's: Fast food down on the island. Perfect for grabbing a quick bite to eat.
  • Blackbeards': Local seafood, burgers, and sandwiches since 1978.
